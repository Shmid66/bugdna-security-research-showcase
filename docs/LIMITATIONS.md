# Limitations and Claim Boundary

## Known limitations

- The Solidity parser remains regex-oriented rather than a full compiler AST.
- Inter-function reasoning is deliberately bounded and a dispatch-helper false negative remains documented.
- Historical and post-change holdouts are curated rather than random representative samples.
- No real-world accuracy percentage, perfect recall, exploitability, or autonomous-audit capability is established.
- No external practitioner testimonial, customer validation, measured time saving, or paid pilot has been obtained.
- Eligibility, authorization, KYC, sanctions, payout accessibility, and current rules require separate evidence for every opportunity.
